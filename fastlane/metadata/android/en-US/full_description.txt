<b>OSMTracker for Android</b> allows you to track your journey and mark significant waypoints. Waypoints can be marked
using a button with a tag, a quick voice recording, a text note, or by taking a picture. The various button pages and
layouts can be customized to suit your OpenStreetMap activities. You can view your current track on top of an
OpenStreetMap background.

<b>Features:</b>
<ul>
  <li><b>GPS Track Logging:</b> Record and store your journey as a GPX track.</li>
  <li><b>Waypoint Marking:</b> Add points of interest with:
    <ul>
      <li>Customizable quick-access buttons</li>
      <li>Voice recordings</li>
      <li>Text notes</li>
      <li>Photos</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li><b>OpenStreetMap Integration:</b> View your GPS track over an OpenStreetMap background.</li>
  <li><b>Offline Usage:</b> Works without an internet connection, ideal for remote areas.</li>
</ul>

Whether you're mapping new territories, hiking, cycling, or exploring, <b>OSMTracker</b> is the perfect tool for
OpenStreetMap contributors and outdoor adventurers.
